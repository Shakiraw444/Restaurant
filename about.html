We're mad about Nigerian food

Our passion for food comes from the African lifestyle where a meal means sitting down with family and friends and sharing laughter, stories and bountiful food. Everyone is welcome into our home where we offer a distinctive relaxed guest experience, serving expertly prepared food from farm and ocean to pan. Generosity is at the core of everything we do and can be felt in our abundant portions, complimentary meals and sauces, and quality, affordable meals.

Nutritional facts and composition are available per item on our menu page providing informed and enhanced dining experience. The Nigerian diet offers variety, flavour and nutrient rich foods for all. As we source a variety of quality ingredients that are responsibly wild caught or farmed all over the world, we are able to offer real value for money meals. We are obsessed with making sure our suppliers are responsible, our product perfect, and our guests ecstatic.
